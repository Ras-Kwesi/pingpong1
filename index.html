Let's Play!

This are the rules of our little game: Ping Pong will count up to your number from 1 with the following exceptions:
  1. Numbers divisible by 3 change to "ping"
  2. Numbers divisible by 5 change to "pong"
  3. Numbers divisible by 15 change to "pingpong"
